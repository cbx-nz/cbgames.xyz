Sheep Farming Calendar

This calendar outlines the key activities and tasks for sheep farming throughout the year.

Month Events
January Monitor pasture growth, check water supply, and prepare for lambing.
February Continue monitoring pasture, start weaning lambs, and check for parasites.
March Shear sheep, vaccinate against clostridial diseases, and prepare for breeding season.
April Start breeding season, monitor ewes for health issues, and manage pasture.
May Continue breeding, check rams for fertility, and manage feed supply.
June Prepare for lambing, ensure ewes are in good condition, and monitor health.
July Lambing season begins, provide extra care to ewes and newborn lambs.
August Continue lambing, monitor lamb growth rates, and manage pasture quality.
September Wean lambs, vaccinate against diseases, and prepare for winter feeding.
October Monitor pasture regrowth, check water supply, and prepare for shearing.
November Shear sheep, check for parasites, and manage feed supply.
December Monitor pasture growth, prepare for the next breeding season.
