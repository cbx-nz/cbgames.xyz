What is Addiction?

Addiction is a complex condition that involves compulsive engagement in rewarding stimuli, despite adverse consequences.

Types of Addictions

Common types of addictions include substance abuse (e.g., alcohol, drugs) and behavioral addictions (e.g., gambling, gaming).

Warning Signs

Warning signs of addiction include withdrawal symptoms, neglecting responsibilities, and an inability to stop.

Getting Help

If you or someone you know struggles with addiction, seek help from medical professionals, counselors, or support groups.
